Islamia Alia Kamil Madrasah

By result, discipline, name and fame, Islamia Alia Kamil Madrasah is one of the best institutions all over the country. Friendly learning environment is the quality of it. The teaching stuff and management of Islamia Alia Kamil Madrasah is efficient,excellent and favourite to the students. Our madrasah was established in 1962 but the result of it is very excellent. The objective of Islamia Alia Kamil Madrasah is to ensure quality education and supply quality manpower for the development of the nation. It is very painful, sorrowful and alarming that the standard of education in our country is very low and questionable. Student politics, campus violence and social unconsciousness are liable and responsible for it. We want to turn out form it and try to give environment for quality education.Al-hamdulillah, in this time we are successful, and hopeful.We want to get help from stockholders and society to fulfill our commitment and target,aim and objectives. Pray for us. Thank you.
